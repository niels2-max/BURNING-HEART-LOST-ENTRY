DESCRIPTION

Henk first entered investigation records following a field interview conducted at his residence.

During the interview, the subject repeatedly referred to two names: BLOKNOOT and BLONKOT.

Henk insisted that the two are separate individuals despite frequently confusing the names during conversation.

The subject also made repeated references to unidentified "files" which he appeared unable or unwilling to fully explain.

RECENT INCIDENT

Subject contacted the investigator by telephone during the evening hours.

Transcript fragment:

"It's the monkey... he's in my walls... Sir Mupp knows... he only watches when you don't..."

Call terminated abruptly.

Subsequent attempts to re-establish contact were unsuccessful.

ANALYST NOTES

Subject exhibits signs of elevated paranoia and confusion.

However, several topics mentioned by the subject have later appeared independently in unrelated investigations.

These include:

• The Monkey
• Sir Mupp
• The Files
• Bloknoot / Blonkot

Reliability of testimony remains undetermined.
