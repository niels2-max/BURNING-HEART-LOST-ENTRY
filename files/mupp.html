DESCRIPTION

Sir Mupp became relevant to the investigation after being referenced during a telephone call placed by Henk.

The subject resides near the border region and has demonstrated knowledge regarding several ongoing investigations.

While generally cooperative with investigators, the subject frequently provides incomplete information.

Motives remain unclear.

FIELD INTERVIEW

Investigators visited the subject's residence following reports connecting him to recent incidents.

During questioning, Sir Mupp was informed of statements made by Henk concerning an entity referred to as "the Monkey."

The subject responded:

"He is hearing it again. It comes and goes."

When asked to elaborate, the subject declined further explanation.

Investigators were later shown a locked room inside the residence.

An unidentified movement was reportedly heard from within.

STATEMENT

Prior to the conclusion of the interview, the subject provided the following guidance:

"The desert is older than you think. And so are the footprints."

The subject later added:

"When you go, don't follow the biggest ones. Follow the ones that stop too suddenly."

ANALYST NOTES

Subject demonstrates familiarity with information not publicly available.

Despite repeated questioning, Sir Mupp consistently avoids direct explanations.

Several statements made by the subject were later found to correspond with observations made in the desert region.

Degree of involvement remains unknown.
