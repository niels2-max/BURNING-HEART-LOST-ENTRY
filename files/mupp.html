DESCRIPTION

Large desert region located near an unidentified border zone.

The location became the focus of investigation following numerous witness reports describing unusual activity.

Official attempts to verify several reported landmarks have produced inconsistent results.

The area remains under active observation.

RECORDED PHENOMENA

Multiple witnesses have reported observing a gigantic yellow chick traversing the region.

Descriptions include movement through cactus fields and sunflower plains.

Several reports describe enormous human footprints appearing throughout the landscape.

Scale of the impressions exceeds any known natural explanation.

FIELD OBSERVATIONS

Investigators documented numerous large disturbances in the sand.

Some footprint trails continued for considerable distances.

Others appeared to terminate abruptly without indication of where the source traveled next.

A single isolated footprint was later discovered with no corresponding trail leading toward or away from the location.

No explanation has been established.

RECOVERED MARKINGS

A symbol was discovered scratched into a rock formation within the investigation area.

Description:

• Circle
• Crown
• Horizontal strike through both symbols

Meaning remains unknown.

ANALYST NOTES

Multiple reports originating from this region contain recurring references to a settlement known as "MUPP."

No official records acknowledge the existence of such a location.

Geographic details supplied by witnesses frequently contradict one another.

Further investigation authorized.
