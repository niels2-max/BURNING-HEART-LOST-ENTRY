DESCRIPTION

The Monkey is an unidentified entity referenced throughout multiple witness statements and interviews.

No verified visual evidence currently exists.

It remains unknown whether the Monkey is a physical entity, a psychological phenomenon, or a recurring misconception shared among unrelated individuals.

HENK INCIDENT

The most detailed report originates from Henk during a late-night telephone call.

According to the subject, the Monkey was located inside the walls of his residence.

Recovered statement fragment:

"It's the monkey... he's in my walls... I can hear him or maybe I can't... I don't know anymore..."

Communication terminated shortly thereafter.

KNOWN STATEMENTS

During a later interview, Sir Mupp was informed of Henk's claims.

Rather than expressing confusion, the subject responded:

"He is hearing it again. It comes and goes."

No further clarification was provided.

This remains the only independent acknowledgment of the Monkey's existence.

OBSERVED CHARACTERISTICS

Information currently attributed to the entity includes:

• Associated with walls or enclosed spaces.
• May produce sounds or perceived movement.
• Not consistently visible.
• Appears to be recognized by multiple individuals.

None of these observations have been independently verified.

ANALYST NOTES

One phrase repeatedly appears in testimony associated with the Monkey:

"He only watches when you don't."

The meaning of this statement remains unknown.

At present, investigators are unable to determine whether the Monkey represents a genuine threat, a symbolic figure, or a misunderstanding of unrelated events.
