LOCATION RECORD

FILE ID LOC-018
TYPE RESTRICTED LOCATION
STATUS ACTIVE INVESTIGATION
REGION BORDER TERRITORY
CLEARANCE LEVEL 2

DESCRIPTION

Large desert region currently under investigation by the Special Investigations Unit.

The location became a priority following repeated reports of unusual landmarks, unexplained entities, and tracks inconsistent with any known animal or human activity.

Geographic surveys have produced inconsistent results.

Certain locations described by witnesses have not been successfully relocated despite multiple search operations.

RECORDED PHENOMENA

Witnesses have reported the presence of a gigantic yellow chick moving through the region.

Additional reports describe extensive sunflower fields and unusually dense cactus growth occurring far from mapped locations.

Several witnesses claim large areas of terrain appeared flattened without an identifiable cause.

None of these reports have been fully explained.

FOOTPRINT INVESTIGATION

Multiple sets of enormous human-like footprints have been documented throughout the region.

The scale of the impressions exceeds that of any known individual.

Several trails continue for considerable distances across open terrain.

Others terminate abruptly without indication of where the source traveled next.

During a recent expedition, investigators followed a trail that ended suddenly in open terrain.

One isolated footprint was later discovered with no trail leading toward or away from the location.

RECOVERED MARKINGS

Investigators discovered an unusual symbol scratched into a rock formation.

The symbol consisted of:

• A circle
• A crown
• A strike passing through both

The meaning of the marking remains unknown.

Similar symbols have appeared in unrelated witness reports.

ENTITY SIGHTINGS

Multiple sightings of an unidentified crowned skeletal figure have been reported within the region.

Witness descriptions remain remarkably consistent despite originating from unrelated individuals.

The entity is frequently described as observing from a distance before disappearing.

No verified photograph or video evidence has been obtained.

ANALYST NOTES

The region remains one of the least understood locations currently under investigation.

Reports originating from the area include accounts of enormous footprints, unexplained markings, and sightings of unidentified entities.

Several observations made within the region appear to correspond with statements provided by Sir Mupp.

Notably, an expedition located a footprint trail that stopped suddenly, matching a warning previously recorded during an interview.

The significance of these correlations remains unknown.

Further investigation authorized.
