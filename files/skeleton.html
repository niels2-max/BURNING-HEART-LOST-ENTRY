THE CROWNED SKELETON

TYPE UNIDENTIFIED ENTITY
STATUS ACTIVE INVESTIGATION
FIRST REPORTED UNKNOWN
CLEARANCE LEVEL 3

DESCRIPTION

The Crowned Skeleton is an unidentified humanoid figure reported by multiple independent witnesses.

Reports consistently describe a skeletal appearance accompanied by a crown-like object positioned above the head.

No confirmed identity has been established.

The entity's purpose, origin, and intentions remain unknown.

OBSERVATIONS

Witnesses have reported sightings in multiple locations.

Confirmed reports place the entity near residential areas, public pathways, and within the desert investigation zone.

In most cases the entity is observed at a distance.

Witnesses frequently report noticing the figure watching them before it disappears from view.

BEHAVIOR

Several reports suggest the entity may be following specific individuals.

The duration and purpose of this behavior remain unknown.

No reports indicate direct communication.

No reports indicate physical aggression.

Despite repeated sightings, the entity has never been successfully approached.

DOCUMENTATION ATTEMPTS

Numerous witnesses claim to have observed the entity.

Despite this, no verified photograph or video recording currently exists.

Investigators have reviewed multiple submissions.

None have been authenticated.

The reason for the lack of reliable visual evidence remains unknown.

ASSOCIATED SYMBOLS

A recurring crown symbol has appeared in several reports connected to the entity.

Similar markings have also been documented within the desert investigation area.

The relationship between the symbol and the entity has not been determined.

ANALYST NOTES

The Crowned Skeleton remains one of the most frequently reported entities within the current investigation.

Unlike most reported phenomena, sightings originate from multiple unrelated witnesses.

The consistency of descriptions across reports is considered unusual.

At present, investigators cannot determine whether the entity is observing individuals intentionally or whether the pattern is coincidental.
